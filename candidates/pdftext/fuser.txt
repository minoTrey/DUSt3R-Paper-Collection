distortion, and measurement noise, FUSER achieves high-quality feed-forward multiview registration, as shown in Fig. 7,
demonstrating strong robustness and generalizability in real-world scenarios. We note that textures are added solely for
clearer visualization; the actual input to FUSER consists only of point-cloud data.
