Fig. 2. Given a monocular video, SHOW reconstructs visible humans and the surrounding scene in a shared 3D world through a single feed-forward pipeline.
We first adapt VGGT with mask prompting and an auxiliary DensePose head to extract human-aware geometry features and predict normalized scene point
maps. A geometry-aware SMPL-X decoder then fuses human features with scene context to estimate body pose, shape, global translation, and a scale factor
that maps the normalized point map to the metric scale of each reconstructed human. Through joint training, SHOW aligns human scale and placement with
the surrounding 3D scene, producing coherent human-scene reconstructions.
