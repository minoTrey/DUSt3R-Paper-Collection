The Long3D [43] dataset contains sequences of up to                  of additional anchors is outweighed by the loss of flexible
10,000 consecutive frames, producing dense point clouds              cache capacity. We therefore adopt Kmax =3 as the default,

the large spatial extent of the reconstructions. We therefore
implement a robust multi-stage evaluation pipeline. First,
the raw dense point clouds are downsampled to a tractable
resolution. Second, Statistical Outlier Removal (SOR) is
applied to suppress noise and spurious points. Third, adap-

the spatial extent of each reconstruction. Fourth, feature-

to-fine) refinement produces the final alignment from which
all metrics are computed.
